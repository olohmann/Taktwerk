Loading & Unloading Jobs

What Does Loading Mean?

A loaded job is registered with launchd and can be started by the system. An unloaded job exists as a plist file but is not active.

Loading a Job

Select a job in the sidebar, then click Load in the detail pane. Taktwerk runs launchctl load to register the job with launchd.

Unloading a Job

Select a loaded job and click Unload. This runs launchctl unload to deregister the job. The plist file remains on disk.

Job Status

The sidebar shows the current status of each job:

  • Loaded — The job is registered with launchd and may be running.
  • Not Loaded — The plist file exists but the job is not registered.
Note: Loading and unloading global agents and daemons may require administrator privileges. Taktwerk uses your user's launchd domain for user agents.

Run At Load

Jobs with RunAtLoad set to true in their plist will start immediately when loaded. You can change this setting in the plist editor.
